About

Since our founding in 1975, Sun Aviation has been a family-owned business. Now in the second generation, we pride ourselves in continuing to stock quality, high-value products and providing superior service to our customers.


Sun only sells to professional aviation customers, and is a member of the Aircraft Electronics Association, the National Business Aviation Association, and Helicopter Association International.


In 2002, Sun was one of the first distributors to be accredited as Full distributors AS-7104 by the Performance Review Institute of the United States, under the National Aerospace Defense Contractors Accreditation Program (NADCAP). This accreditation goes beyond the ISO 9000 requirements of quality assurance.
